England, Wales and Scotland Ethnicity 2011

This page shows in interactive chart form the population ethnicities from the 2011 censuses.

The percentages of the populations are shown on the charts below. Please note there is no distinction on the census of Scotland between "white" and "Scottish white".'

Although Ethnicity does not necessarily show a Haplogroup link because it is goes on a best fit or best guess within the censuses there is a high probability that if persons in the census had a DNA test on the Y-DNA there would be strong reason to suspect that a persons ethnicity is linked to a regional Haplogroup. But stating a ethnicity does not nessecrily mean that persons DNA comes from a single point of origin.
